Welcome to DONATURE!

DONATURE is a fundraising platform bringing together nonprofit organisations and social entrepreneurs working to uplift our planet and people. Our expert team puts in the work to find and vet legitimate projects around the world that are in need of financial or voluntary assistance. The frequently updated DONATURE map makes it easy for donors and volunteers to find projects to which they would be willing to donate their time or money.
