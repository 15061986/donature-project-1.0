Who we are

DONATURE is made up of a young and dynamic team of social entrepreneurs, web developers, nonprofit executives and data scientists who are dedicated to creating a sustainable world. We are driven by a passion for the planet and its people, the vision of a fair and equal world, and the mission to make giving back to others accessible.
There's no longer a need to waste your time scouring the web for social impact projects and organisations. We offer an all-in-one solution to review organisations and choose to donate or volunteer. Our partners are genuine and reliable, and operate in a range of global settings from war zones to corporate boardrooms. You can trust us to help you find a project close to your heart.
